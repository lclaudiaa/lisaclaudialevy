Fun fact

Colorful clothing collection that I sewed:

During the first lockdown of 2020, I needed a way to express my frustration towards the pandemic, so I started to sew a clothing line out of old recycled lab coats and any day-to-day objects such as paper, placemats, face masks, and more. I wanted to show that we could add life and joy to anything. Even if the original website is in French, you can check out the video clip that I made!
