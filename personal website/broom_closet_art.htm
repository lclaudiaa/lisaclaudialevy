ART INSTALLATION ON SOCIETY'S JUDGEMENT

After being bullied at school I decided to create an art installation in the broom closet of my High School's cafeteria to talk about society's judgement. Every person would get dressed as a prisoner, and enter into this broom closet installation for 3 min.
