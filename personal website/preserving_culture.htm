Presersing Culture In University of Geneva

In 2022, I founded the first association on the judaic culture of University of Geneva, that we call ACJ. We designed acticvities to promote the deeper understanding of diverse cultures. We organised workshops for peer students to teach how to embrace religious tolerance, understanding, acceptance and a willingness to move beyond our religious and cultural differences. We curated intercultural dialogue conferences / events to make participating students more familiar with the jewish religion and the Shoah.
